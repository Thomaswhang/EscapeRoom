♖ Chess is a strategic board game between two players.
The goal is to checkmate the opponent. What does that mean? The opponent's king is threatened and unable to escape.
Each player has 16 pieces including pawns, rooks, horses, runners, a king and a queen.
Each piece has its own movement rules.

WARNING!

♖ The Tower only moves horizontally
♗ The Rooks only diagonally
♕ The Queen both horizontal and diagonal
♘ The Horse in L-shape
♔ The King in every direction except one step
♙ The Pawn moves in forward direction one step (two-step in the first move)
